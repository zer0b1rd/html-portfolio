ZeroBird's favorite movies


My Top 3 movies of all-time.


Spirited Away

The story of Spirited Away is just amazing, the visuals are stunning and the whole anime is a masterpiece.


Ex Machina

Is a super interesting and beautiful movie about an experiment with a female human A.I.
It raises moral questions about when a human being is a human being. It is my favorite movie that is not an anime.


Princess Mononoke

Princess Mononoke is an incredibly beautiful, but also deeply serious anime, which addresses important points of society/humanity.
I've probably seen this movie over 15 times and it never gets bad or boring.
